Prologue

My passion for image processing, and computer vision developed while enrolled in a graduate data visualization course at Southern Methodist University in the summer of 2016. However, my passion for music and the arts emerged long before graduate school; I began building websites, and storing and indexing digital media, games, and software at a very young age. My interest in music fused with my love for visualizing data to create thoughtful masterpieces generatively through code. MusicMind, is a project that is a culmination of my deep love for learning, computing, and art.
