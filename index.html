Prologue

My passion for image processing, and machine learning developed while enrolled in a graduate data visualization course at Southern Methodist University in the summer of 2016. However, my passion for music and the arts emerged long before graduate school; I began building websites, and storing and indexing digital media, games, and software at a very young age. My interest in music fused with my love for visualizing data; and so I began creating thoughtful masterpieces, generatively through code. I strive to build powerful, and fun software, and MusicMind is a culmination of my life's work, that is continually refreshed by my curiosity in computing and art.
