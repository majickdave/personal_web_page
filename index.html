David Samuel

All my life I have had a passion in music creation, discovery, and sharing, but had no way to truly express myself as a Sound Arranger. My pursuit of making music shifted to a desire to create artful music experiences. My skillset coupled with knowledge and access to the powerful tools made available by Silicon Valley giants, I have created a simple solution to my problem, that has vast implications for music, video, augmented reality, and social networking. MusicMind Inc. is inspired by you, the listener, who is looking for a deeper connection to the artists and content creators that you love.
